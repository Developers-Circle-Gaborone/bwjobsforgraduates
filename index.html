Mission

Bw Jobs 4 Graduates stands to prevent and cure unemployment amongst the youth of Botswana , the organization prepares and equip the youth with leadership skills, business skills, self-responsibility and encouraging entrepreneurship among the youth to prepare them to effectively and productively respond to the challenges of school, university, the corporate world, labour market and society at large

The Organisation's objectives are

1. To help each other as graduates in finding jobs anywhere around the country. 2. To help those who does not have access to newspapers by posting job adverts for each other. 3. To share Cover letter writing skills, CV writing skills and interview skills. 4. To share job interview experiences 5. To share those jobs that are usually advertised on company newsletters e.g Debswana, BCL e.t.c 6. To uplift each other when the going seems to be getting tough
