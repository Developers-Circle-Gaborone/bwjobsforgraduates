Company Overview


Non-profit organisation
Bw Jobs 4 Graduates (BWJ4G) is a Youth Led Organization registered under the Ministry of Labor and Home Affairs (Section 6(1) of the Societies Act advocating for Youth Employment, Empowerment and Development. The Organisation is an affiliate of the Botswana National Youth Council (BNYC).
The organization acts as a remedy for addressing the escalating unemployment issues among youth in general and also enhances the spirit of positive participation amongst the entire nation towards helping those in search of employment by availing direction for vacancies available in areas that are out of their reach.
This is driven by young people, sharing ideas, opportunities and experiences for the betterment of the youth of Botswana.
Bw Jobs 4 Graduates believes in sharing and thus its goal is to expose those who are less privileged opportunities that are relevant to their individual personal empowerment and growth.
The organization is made up of three phases being the student phase which targets school going individuals from Primary School level to Tertiary education level, the Graduate phase which is the core mandate of the organization which caters for recent graduates and the Young Professionals Phase which targets the young professionals from both government and private institutions.

General Information


BW Jobs 4 Graduates is an organisation formed by young inspired, motivated and determined Batswana graduates who want to make a positive contribution in other graduates life!
The Organisation's objectives are:

1. To help each other as graduates in finding jobs anywhere around the country.
2. To help those who does not have access to newspapers by posting job adverts for each other.
3. To share Cover letter writing skills, CV writing skills and interview skills.
4. To share job interview experiences
5. To share those jobs that are usually advertised on company newsletters e.g Debswana, BCL e.t.c
6. To uplift each other when the going seems to be getting tough

Requirements when you post vacancies on BW Jobs 4 Graduates:
1. Quote vacancy number/reference (where applicable). 2. Vacancy name. 3. Requirements (qualifications and experience). 4. Deadline. 5. Newspaper (Name, date)

From time to time admins will make researches on latest cv writing skills, interview skills e.t.c and post the info.
